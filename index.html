PUBLIC WORKS 🌐

Public Works is an open source ecosystem fund focused on constructing essential public infrastructure. We fundamentally believe that the code we create can be sustainable while being generative to the broader communities it serves.

Our community consists of builders with strong principles, vision, and a deep belief that the world needs better, more internet-native tools to coordinate around 21st century problems.

In the past, we've relied on centralized institutions to provision the roads, bridges, and electrical grids our cities rely on. Now, we can use decentralized systems to rely on each other.

Public goods are good.
